Our Story

Residential
First. Always.

Fix & Go was founded with a clear focus: residential homes only. Unlike larger contractors who split their attention between commercial and domestic work, we dedicate everything to homeowners.

We understand that managing a home can feel overwhelming — from finding the right tradesperson to trusting someone in your space. That's why we built a business around reliability, transparency and care.

Every member of the Fix & Go team is vetted, trained to the latest industry standards and shares our commitment to leaving every home better than we found it.
